## Decisions

- **Task 4.3 split into 3 files.** The subagent's initial output was 436 lines in one file, over the ~300-line project limit. Split: HangsButton (189), MicButton (129), ProgressBarView (99). The handoff named a single `ComponentInspectorTests.swift` but splitting is the right call per `feedback_file_size_limit.md`.
- **PaywallView "unlocked" variant doesn't exist.** The audit A2-3 described "locked vs unlocked" but `PaywallView` has no success/unlocked state ‚Äî once unlocked, the paywall is dismissed. Chosen variants instead: (A) `limitError` present + product loaded = countdown + price button, (B) `limitError` nil + product nil = no countdown + loading button. Both are structurally distinct under `.dump`.
- **`CustomReflectable` on mock, not production code.** The non-deterministic `.dump` was caused by AsyncStream internals reachable via Mirror. Adding `CustomReflectable` to `MockPurchaseService` (a `#if DEBUG`-gated file) avoids touching any production surface. Future `.dump` tests that transitively reach this mock will also benefit.
- **Streaming tests were NOT broken by Phase 4.** They failed in one full-suite run due to main-actor contention under Swift Testing's parallel runner; they pass consistently in isolation and in subsequent full-suite runs. No fix needed.
