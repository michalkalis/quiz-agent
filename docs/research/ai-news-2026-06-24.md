## 1. OpenRouter subagent server tool — delegate cheap work mid-generation

**Source:** [OpenRouter on X (announcement)](https://x.com/OpenRouter/status/2065602438439043469) · [Subagent blog post — OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai/blog/announcements/subagent-server-tool/)

Announced June 16, 2026. Adding `openrouter:subagent` to the tools array lets a frontier orchestrator hand off self-contained sub-tasks (structured extraction, reformatting, summarisation) to any cheaper worker model mid-generation. Worker tokens bill at the worker model's rates. Recursion is blocked; max 10 delegations per request.

**Relevance to us:** Our question-generation pipeline runs a best-of-N loop where expensive frontier calls (gpt-4o) also handle JSON extraction and formatting. The `openrouter:subagent` tool could push those extraction/parse steps to a cheap worker (e.g. gpt-4o-mini or a free model) while the orchestrator stays on the frontier tier. Similarly, our multi-model scoring stage in `apps/quiz-pack-api` could delegate boilerplate steps. Not verified against our gateway toggle (LLM_GATEWAY) — needs a quick compatibility check. **Action: open an issue to evaluate and prototype.**
