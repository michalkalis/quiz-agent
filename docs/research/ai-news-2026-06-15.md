## 1. OpenRouter Unified Audio APIs — MAI-Transcribe 1.5 supports Slovak

**Sources:**
- [New Audio APIs for Speech and Transcription — OpenRouter Blog](https://openrouter.ai/blog/announcements/announcing-audio-apis/)
- [MAI-Transcribe 1.5 on OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai/microsoft/mai-transcribe-1.5)
- [Introducing MAI-Transcribe-1.5 — Microsoft AI](https://microsoft.ai/news/mai-transcribe-1-5more-accurate-context-aware-and-built-for-production/)
- [MAI-Transcribe-1.5 Review — AIToolTier](https://aitooltier.com/tools/mai-transcribe-1)

**Summary:** On May 1, 2026, OpenRouter added `/api/v1/audio/transcriptions` and `/api/v1/audio/speech` endpoints — the same routing, billing, and key management as text calls. Available models: Whisper Large V3, GPT-4o Mini Transcribe, Voxtral Mini Transcribe, and (since June 2) Microsoft MAI-Transcribe 1.5. MAI-Transcribe 1.5 supports 43 languages including Slovak, priced at $0.36/hr, and can transcribe 1 hour of audio in under 15 seconds. Whisper-1 on OpenRouter is also now listed for comparison.

**Relevance to us:** We call `whisper-1` directly via OpenAI for Slovak quiz-answer transcription. MAI-Transcribe 1.5's explicit Slovak support and speed may be meaningfully better for our primary language. Routing STT through OpenRouter would also consolidate billing under the LLM_GATEWAY toggle. **Suggested action: benchmark MAI-Transcribe 1.5 vs Whisper-1 on a sample of Slovak quiz answers; if accuracy improves, switch STT through OpenRouter.** This would warrant a new issue — see note below.

## 2. OpenRouter Fusion API — compound multi-model judge, fully launched

**Sources:**
- [OpenRouter on X — Fusion announcement](https://x.com/OpenRouter/status/2065856853989270011)
- [OpenRouter Fusion API: Fable-Level at Half the Price — modemguides](https://www.modemguides.com/blogs/ai-news/openrouter-fusion-compound-ai-fable-alternative)
- [What Is OpenRouter Fusion? — MindStudio](https://www.mindstudio.ai/blog/what-is-openrouter-fusion-multi-model-api)
- [Fusion — OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai/openrouter/fusion)

**Summary:** On June 13, 2026, OpenRouter fully launched its Fusion API (model ID `openrouter/fusion`). It fans a prompt out to 3–5 models simultaneously, then a judge model synthesizes the panel responses. Pricing = sum of underlying token costs + judge tokens; no subscription. On a 100-task hard research benchmark, Fusion matched Claude Fable 5 (now suspended); a budget panel (Gemini 3 Flash + Kimi K2.6 + DeepSeek V4 Pro) hit 64.7% quality at roughly half the cost of a single top-tier call. Timing coincided with Fable 5's suspension (see item 4).

**Relevance to us:** We hand-roll a best-of-N question generation pipeline with an LLM critique judge, and run a multi-model A/B scoring experiment (claude-sonnet-4-6 + gpt-4.1-mini). Fusion is a drop-in API that replaces custom fan-out logic. The "judge model" synthesis step maps exactly to our use case. **Suggested action: run a cost/quality comparison of Fusion vs our current judge pipeline on a batch of question candidates.** This would warrant a new issue — see note below.

## 3. Gemini 3.5 Flash — ~10x price increase; audit our fact-verification pipeline

**Sources:**
- [Gemini 3.5 Flash: more expensive — Simon Willison](https://simonwillison.net/2026/May/19/gemini-35-flash/)
- [Gemini 3.5 Flash complete guide — CodersEra](https://codersera.com/blog/gemini-3-5-complete-guide-2026/)
- [Gemini 3.5 Flash — OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai/google/gemini-3.5-flash)
- [AI Hallucination Rates 2026 — Suprmind](https://suprmind.ai/hub/ai-hallucination-rates-and-benchmarks/)

**Summary:** Launched May 19, 2026 at Google I/O. Pricing: $1.50 input / $9.00 output per 1M tokens — roughly 10× Gemini 2.5 Flash's rates. Quality improved: hallucination rate dropped 31 percentage points vs Gemini 3 Flash (to 61%), though frontier leaders like Grok 4.3 and MiMo-V2.5-Pro sit at 25%. Strong on agentic benchmarks (MCP Atlas: 83.6%). Gemini 3.5 Pro is expected GA June 2026.

**Relevance to us:** We call Gemini for fact + logical verification. If our model string is unversioned (e.g. `gemini-flash-latest`), the API may have silently upgraded to 3.5 Flash, inflating costs ~10×. The quality improvement (31-pt hallucination drop) may or may not justify the price for a fact-check pipeline that already uses Tavily web search as the primary signal. **Suggested action: immediately audit `apps/quiz-pack-api` to confirm which Gemini model ID is hardcoded; pin to `gemini-2.5-flash` if 3.5 Flash costs are not budgeted.** Watch only for now unless audit reveals we're already on 3.5 Flash.
