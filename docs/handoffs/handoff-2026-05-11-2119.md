## Decisions

**Revised UX bet pairing for 6 directions** (settled in this session — don't re-litigate without a reason):

| # | Visual direction | UX bet | Voice/Touch |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Claude editorial calm (cream/terracotta/serif) | Solo reflexný režim, manual Next, explanation expand, no countdown | Touch primary |
| 2 | Swiss Modernist (white/crimson/grid) | Power-user dashboard, stats always visible in side rail | Touch |
| 3 | Neo-brutalist Pop (butter/coral/hard shadows) | Game-show, combos, shareable result card, multiplayer-ready | Touch/tap MCQ |
| 4 | Liquid Glass dark (navy/neon/blur) | Hands-free voice console — driving variant, always-listening | Voice primary |
| 5 | Soft Pastel Wellness (sage/peach/24px) | Daily ritual, 5Q habit, no time pressure | Touch, slow |
| 6 | Editorial Newspaper (off-white/serif/rule lines) | Magazine reader, "Today's Issue", long-form trivia, educational | Touch/scroll |

**Why:** original plan tested only visual taste, not UX. Pairings are chosen so that choosing between directions = choosing between product bets.

**Screens per direction:** Home + Quiz active + Result-correct + Result-incorrect (4, not 3). Result-incorrect added because the emotional tone on failure is a real differentiator and the pairings above have different answers to that.

**iPhone frame:** 393×852 (iPhone 15/16), not 375×812. Modern Dynamic Island chrome.

**5★ rating removed from main Result flow** — placed in post-session recap only (where applicable). Auto-advance countdown only in directions #3 and #4; manual "Next" in all others.

**Compact-compare grids added at end of HTML:** 6× Quiz-active side-by-side and 6× Result-correct side-by-side without iPhone chrome. This is the only reliable way to evaluate visual strength across directions.
