## 4. Claude Fable 5 — watch only

**Source:** [Anthropic official docs](https://platform.claude.com/docs/en/about-claude/models/introducing-claude-fable-5-and-claude-mythos-5) · [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/09/anthropic-mythos-claude-fable-5.html)

Launched June 9; model ID `claude-fable-5`. Pricing: $10/$50 per 1M tokens. 1M-token context. Includes safety classifiers that can return `stop_reason: "refusal"` — a new response shape to handle. Note: a US government export-control directive issued ~June 12 suspended access to both Fable 5 and Mythos 5; status unclear as of this writing.

**Relevance to us:** We use `claude-sonnet-4-6` (unaffected by the suspension). Fable 5 is too expensive ($50/M output) for our scoring pipeline. The export-control situation is worth monitoring if we ever consider upgrading the scoring judge. **Watch only.**
